Your invisible Smart Home.

The ubisys Smart Home primarily is a flush-mounted solution. The components can be retrofitted – and disappear hidden away behind your switches or push buttons.

Installation

ubisys flush-mounted components can be installed behind any light switch, push button, or socket as well as wall or ceiling outlet – No need to renew your existing switches and sockets. The particularly compact design simplifies the installation.

Advantages

Minimal size

Due to miniaturization the devices can be retrofitted easily .

Wiring

No additional wiring required. This saves cost and time.

Flexibility

When retrofitting you already are flexible during the planning phase.

Switch product ranges

The components can be installed regardless to switch product ranges.

Individual retrofitting

Upgrade your system bit by bit with additional components. Depending on requirements and budget.

Rail mounted devices

The flush-mounted versions are also available as DIN rail mounted devices for the control cabinet.

This is how your ubisys Smart Home is set up.

The intelligent system from ubisys connects your electrical appliances wirelessly. This way, multiple devices can be controlled and monitored simultaneously. Via a connection to the Internet, access on the go is also possible.

Here’s how it works:

1. Connect gateway

Link the ubisys gateway to your DSL / UMTS / LTE router.

2. Download App

Download the ubisys app and Connect it to your gateway.

3. Installation of components

Install smart home components for your intelligent home.

4. Setting up your smart home

After installation, configure your smart home according to your ideas using the ubisys app.
